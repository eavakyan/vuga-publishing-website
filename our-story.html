Our Story

VUGA Publishing was built on a simple belief: every writer deserves the opportunity to share their stories with the world.

Mission

The book is the easy part. The press is the hard part.

Every indie author can upload a manuscript to Amazon. The thing that actually decides whether a book gets read is press — and indie authors don't have the magazine relationships to get any. That's the gap VUGA Publishing was built to close.

We're a marketing-first publisher. Our specialty is one thing — getting your book featured as full editorial articles in major celebrity magazines, on real magazine domains, with permanent indexed URLs. TIME, Rolling Stone UK, Closer Weekly, In Touch Weekly, Star Magazine, OK! Magazine, and Hollywood Life — all by contract.

Plus our 104-outlet owned digital network through VUGA Enterprises. No pitching, no royalty paperwork, no waiting on traditional publishing timelines. Pay online, fill the book-info form, articles go live.

The Network

One platform. Three companies.

VUGA Publishing is part of an integrated media ecosystem designed to give every author maximum reach.

VUGA Publishing

The marketing engine — author packages from $97 trial article to $14,997 Authority tier. Self-service. No calls. No royalty paperwork.

VUGA Media Group & VUGA PR Services

Sister PR and marketing agencies — vugaprservices.com and vugamediagroup.com. Mainstream-media placement and campaign strategy for our authors.

VUGA Enterprises

Owns 104 digital newspapers and magazines. Editorial reach, reader audience, and the underlying network behind every Spark, Bestseller, and Authority package.
