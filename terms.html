The VUGA Promise — 100% Placement Guarantee

Every editorial article in your package is guaranteed by contract. If we fail to publish a placement listed in your purchased package on the named outlet within the stated delivery window, you get a full refund of the corresponding portion of your order — no fine print, no fight, no questions.

This is the foundation of every package we sell. If we don't deliver, you don't pay.

We do not provide traditional retail-distribution services or royalty accounting on author-supplied titles.

  • Spark — $997 (one-time) or Spark Annual — $9,997/year. Spark delivers 10 placements across the VUGA Enterprises digital network (standard outlets).   • Bestseller — $7,997. Spark + full editorial articles in Closer Weekly, In Touch Weekly, and Star Magazine.
  • Authority — $14,997. Bestseller + full editorial articles in OK! Magazine and Hollywood Life.
  • Premium magazine add-ons: Rolling Stone UK ($8,997), TIME ($14,997), or both bundled ($19,997 — saves $3,997).

"Standard outlets" refers to the digital newspapers and magazines in the VUGA Enterprises network (network.html). Premium TV brands and tier-1 magazine add-ons are sold separately and are never bundled into Trial, Spark, Bestseller, or Authority unless explicitly stated above.

3. What Your Placement Guarantee Covers

Each placement in your package is delivered as a full original editorial article (typically 600–1,200 words), written by our editorial team and published on the listed magazine's actual digital domain. Placements are guaranteed by our existing distribution agreements with the named outlets. All placements are digital editions; we do not place into print issues, even for outlets that still print.

What is guaranteed:

  • The placement itself — the published URL on the named magazine's domain.
  • An original editorial article written to that magazine's editorial standards.
  • A permanent, indexable live page (not a temporary pre-press URL).
  • A backlink to your retailer page (Amazon, etc.).
  • Delivery within the published delivery window for your tier.

If any of the above fails for a placement, you receive a full refund of that placement's portion of your order, or — at your election — we re-place the article on another outlet of equivalent or greater tier within 30 days at no cost to you.

4. What's Outside Our Guarantee

The placement is what we control. Sales of your book are not — they depend on your cover, hook, genre fit, retailer ranking, reviews, pricing, and reader behavior, none of which VUGA controls. We do not guarantee specific sales numbers, retailer rankings, royalty income, social-media follow-on, viral pickup, or any commercial outcome downstream of the placement. Press placement is one factor in a book's success — and we deliver it on time, every time. The rest is up to you and the market.

We make no promise that any individual placement will be the breakthrough moment — but the placements happen, and they happen at the outlet you paid for. That's the contract.

All packages are sold self-service online. Payment is processed at checkout. We accept major credit and debit cards. Spark Annual is billed in full at the time of purchase; it is not a recurring subscription that auto-renews — you choose to renew (or not) at the end of the 12-month term.

You agree to provide accurate billing information. Chargebacks initiated without first contacting us at info@vugapublishing.com may result in account suspension and forfeiture of any in-progress placements.

Standard delivery times from the moment your book-info form is fully submitted:

  • Bestseller / Authority: 30 days.
  • Premium magazine add-ons (TIME, Rolling Stone UK): 30–45 days, depending on the outlet's editorial calendar.
  • Premium TV-brand add-ons: 30 days.

Delivery starts when we have your final book information (cover image, retailer link, and a 200-word synopsis). We will notify you by email when each placement is live and include the URL.
